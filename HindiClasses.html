Online Classes:
I provide online classes and these classes are group classes with students of the same level. These are classes that take place over Google Meet.

Onsite Classes:
I provide onsite classes as well and this is also a group class with students of the same level. Go to the contact page and contact me about the address of onsite classes if you are interested. Similar to online classes, these classes are also group classes with students of the same level.

I also provide conversational classes for students who are only interested in learning how to speak in Hindi.
These classes are helpful if you want to learn how to converse with others in Hindi and talk about everyday objects and situations.
