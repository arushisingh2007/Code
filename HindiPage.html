Interested in helping your child learn Hindi while living in the U.S.?
These Hindi Parichay classes can help your child learn how to write, read, and
speak fluently in Hindi. We have classes for every level of students that your child

for students for over 7 years and Hindi Parichay uses their own material and
curriculum which ensures that students are immersed in the language and can
learn how to read, speak, and write Hindi.
