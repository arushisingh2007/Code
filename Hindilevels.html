Hindi Levels Hindi Classes

Hindi Parichay has several levels that students fall under. Check the descriptions below to see which Hindi level your child should take.

Beginner: Students in this level will learn the basics of Hindi such as the alphabet, vowels, consonants, numbers, and more. They will be able to learn basic vocabulary such as numbers, greetings, colors, fruits, vegetables, days of the week, and many more common objects. Students will also learn basic grammatical structure of sentences and verb conjugation. At the end of this level, students will be able to engage in basic Hindi conversations, create their own sentences, and identify everyday objects around them.

Intermediate: Students in the intermediate level will expand their vocabulary to more complex everyday situations and increase their knowledge of sentence structure and verb conjugations. They will also be able to read longer stories and also write more complex setences. Students will also be able to speak more fluently and confidentally as well.

Advanced: Students who are in the advanced level will refine their speaking skills by participating in in-depth conversatoins. They will also be able to read novels along with being able to write essays. Students will focus on advanced grammar structures and focus on complex sentence patterns.
